Monster Survivors

Battle endless waves of monsters, collect power-ups, and survive as long as you can in this action-packed adventure.

Game Features

  • Action-packed gameplay with waves of increasingly difficult monsters
  • Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities and weapons
  • Multiple character classes with unique skills and abilities
  • Real-time leader boards to compete with players worldwide

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map. Automatically attack nearby monsters as they approach.

Defeat monsters to gain experience and level up. Each level-up allows you to select a new perk or weapon upgrade.

Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. The longer you survive, the higher your score!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game where your objective is to survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As you defeat enemies, you'll gain experience and unlock powerful upgrades that enhance your abilities.

The game features various environments and monster types that increase in difficulty as you progress. Strategic decision-making is crucial as you choose which upgrades to prioritize based on your playstyle and the current challenges you face.

With its addictive gameplay loop and progression system, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment as you try to beat your previous records and climb the global leaderboards. Can you become the ultimate survivor?
